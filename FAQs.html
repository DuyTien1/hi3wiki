Honkai Impact 3rd is a free-to-play 3D action RPG mobile game developed by miHoYo. It features a deep story, hack-and-slash combat, and various game modes including single-player and multiplayer content.

You can download the game from your device's app store (iOS/Android) or PC client. After installation, create an account and follow the tutorial to learn basic gameplay mechanics.
